Who We Are

Ricky Mitsuba AI Lab is a pioneering research and development organization dedicated to exploring the boundaries of artificial intelligence and immersive digital experiences. Founded in Kolkata, West Bengal, our lab brings together creative minds, technical experts, and storytellers to craft unique digital journeys that challenge perception and engage the imagination.

Our signature project, The Dark Journey, represents our commitment to creating interactive experiences that blur the line between technology and art, offering users a unique path through darkness, choices, and self-discovery.

Our Mission

To push the boundaries of digital storytelling and artificial intelligence, creating immersive experiences that provoke thought, evoke emotion, and challenge conventional narratives. We believe in the power of interactive media to explore complex themes and create meaningful connections with users.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in AI-driven interactive experiences, blending cutting-edge technology with human creativity to craft digital journeys that resonate on a deeply personal level. We envision a future where technology and art seamlessly merge to create transformative experiences.

Our History

Ricky Mitsuba AI Lab was founded in 2025 in Kolkata with a small team of passionate researchers and creators. What began as an experimental project exploring the intersection of AI and interactive storytelling has evolved into a full-fledged laboratory dedicated to pushing the boundaries of digital experiences.

Over the years, we've developed numerous prototypes and experimental projects, each building on our understanding of how users engage with AI-driven narratives. The Dark Journey represents our most ambitious project to date, incorporating years of research and development into a cohesive, immersive experience.
