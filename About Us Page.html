Our Story

Ricky Mitsuba AI Studio was founded on August 28, 2025, with a simple yet powerful vision: to make complex calculations accessible to everyone. What started as a small project has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving users worldwide.


Founded by Ricky Mitsuba in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, our journey began when he noticed the gap between available calculation tools and real-world needs. We believe that technology should simplify life, not complicate it. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating intuitive, accurate, and reliable calculators that cater to various aspects of daily life.


Today, we're proud to offer 12+ high-end tools, each designed with precision and user experience in mind. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously improve and expand our offerings, ensuring that our users always have access to the best tools available.

Our Mission

Our main motive is to provide an all-in-one solution for all tools under one shed. We strive to democratize access to powerful calculation tools and AI-driven insights, empowering individuals to make informed decisions in their personal, financial, and professional lives.


We are committed to:

  • Providing accurate and reliable calculation tools
  • Making financial planning accessible to everyone
  • Promoting health and wellness through data-driven insights
  • Supporting education with powerful learning tools
  • Continuously innovating and improving our services
