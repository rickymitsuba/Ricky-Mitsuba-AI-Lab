Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your calculators?

Our calculators are built with precision and accuracy in mind. We use industry-standard formulas and regularly update our algorithms to ensure reliable results. However, for critical financial or health decisions, we recommend consulting with professionals.

Are your tools free to use?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use. We believe in making essential calculation tools accessible to everyone. There are no hidden charges or premium features.

Can I suggest new tools or features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. You can use the contact form above to suggest new tools or features. We review all suggestions and prioritize based on user demand.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and we don't store any personal information unless you explicitly provide it through our contact form.

Do you offer API access?

We're currently working on API access for developers. If you're interested in integrating our calculators into your application, please contact us with your requirements.
